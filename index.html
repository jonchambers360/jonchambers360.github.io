Illuminated Targets is an agile and responsive consultancy firm, with deep expertise in pharmaceutical target biology.

We provide bespoke, cost effective solutions to a wide variety of the target-related issues around the process of progressing towards and beyond a New Drug Application.

Typically, for a target, or lists of targets, we build datasets and reports to inform and support target investment decisions: from target prioritization and risk mitigation, to the identification of novel therapeutic opportunities.

Also, in addition to providing data and expertise for the formal submission of documents to regulatory bodies, we can also mine for datasets to address very specific research questions for which a client may not be equipped to mine for themselves.

To deliver these services we rely upon both our deep domain knowledge of target biology, and our internally developed modern AI-enabled pipelines to mine the public biomedical literature and databases.

And for projects which extend beyond the target-centric expertise within Illuminated Targets, we are able to call upon a network of consultants: from toxicologists and chemists through to bioinformaticians and statisticians.
